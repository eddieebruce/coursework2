Batman: Arkham Knight



Summary: Batman: Arhkam Knight is an action-adventure game that is loosly based on the comic book mythos. The game's main storyline follows Batman as he confronts Scarecrow, who has launched an attack on Gothan City, causing a city-wide evacuation. Scarecrow, with the help of the mysterious Arkham Knight, is also able to unite Batman's greatest foes in an attempt to finally destroy Batman.
