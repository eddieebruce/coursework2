Yoshi's Woolly World



Summary: Yoshi's Woolly World is a side-scrolling platformer game that takes place on a knitted island filled with many Yarn Yoshis of varying patterns. The evil magikoopa Kamek turns nearly all the yoshis into bundles of yarn for his master baby bowser, scattering them accross diffrent worlds. However, two yoshis manage to avoid being transformed themselves, and they set off to pursue Kamek and resucue their woolly friends.



Release Year: 26/06/2015



Developer: Good-Feel



Platform(s): Wii U
